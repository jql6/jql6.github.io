Intro to LaTeX

Intro

LaTeX is great for writing math electronically. LaTeX is generally pronounced as "lay-teck" or "lah-teck"; I prefer "lay-teck". LaTeX is able to easily do lots of math things easily such as subscripts within subscripts, matrices, and switch between math notation and English text fairly easily.

Brief note: I am on Mac, so this guide will be specific to Mac users.

Stationery

Stationery are templates for LaTeX. As a beginner, it's best to start out with a good stationery so you can get to using LaTeX right away without fiddling with setup issues. For me, these files are located in:
